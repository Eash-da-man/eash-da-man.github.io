About Me

My hate for coding started when I was 10. I hated the fact that I would be sitting around in a chair staring at a screen for hours as work. I only began to understand the art of programming when I saw the potential it had to create amazing systems and games. It is my dream to create a successful game that I can enjoy with my friends and family. I find it interesting that we can create systems that can connect other people from around the world, allowing them to interact in ways we never could have imagined.

In my current highschool career (as a 9th grader), I have successfully created a brief and (I like to think) fun interactive fiction game. Utilizing my resources and collaborating with my team, we came up with the drafts of what would be Ink and Blood. I have taken Computer Science Essentials as a freshman and am looking forward to be taking AP Computer Science Principles my sophomore year. Achieving the Principal Honor Roll multiple times during my middle school career, I am very good at working with deadlines.

I always strive for best quality work no matter how many long nights (and days) it takes. There have been many passionate and sleepless nights I have been through to produce a program that I would like to believe is foolproof. I think that it is suffice to say that I am persistent, and hardworking. But if there is one weakness that I have, it would be that I tend to be a perfectionist, attempting to correct every little mistake that doesn't seem to affect the project at all. I could work on devoting more time on other projects rather than zoning in on every tiny detail, such as the exact spacing anc color of the font, an excessively organized layout for the code, and other tiny details that I could gone without.

I hope that you were able to get to know me better, although an interview would be more effective! Thank you for reading my about me page and I hope you enjoy my works!
